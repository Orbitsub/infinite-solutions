Tips
Balance Rule

Each AIF needs exactly 1 BIF of Water + 1 BIF of Electrolytes. Each BIF produces 40 P1/hr — the exact demand of one AIF cycle.

Extractor Timing

Shorter extractor cycles yield higher P0 rates but need more attention. Match cycle length to how often you can log in — 23–48 hr cycles are common for passive setups.

Planet Types

Aqueous Liquids and Ionic Solutions are on different planet types — a 2-planet setup is not possible. Minimum is 3 planets (2 extraction + 1 factory).

Customs Tax

Export P1 or higher rather than P0 to reduce total customs office tax paid per unit of Coolant produced.
